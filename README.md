# Coca-Cola DCF - vF

A fully built three-scenario DCF model for The Coca-Cola Company (KO), constructed
from publicly available financials.

## What the model covers

- Five-year revenue and EBIT projections across conservative, base, and optimistic cases
- Dynamic scenario switch that updates all assumptions simultaneously including
  revenue growth, EBIT margin, WACC, and terminal growth rate
- Mid-year discounting convention for greater accuracy
- EBIAT-based unlevered free cash flow build with D&A, CapEx, and normalised NWC
- Gordon Growth Model terminal value
- Bridge from enterprise value to implied share price via net debt and diluted shares
- Sensitivity table across WACC and terminal growth rate
- Dedicated WACC tab: CAPM-derived cost of equity, after-tax cost of debt,
  market-value weights

## Assumptions

Historical financials sourced from public filings. Forward assumptions such as growth rates,
margin expansion, and discount rates are all my own estimates built across
three scenarios to reflect a range of outcomes.
